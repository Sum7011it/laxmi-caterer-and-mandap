About us

Welcome to Seven Seasons Lawn, where every celebration finds its perfect setting amidst the lush greenery and elegant surroundings of our venue. Nestled in the heart of Unique Gardens, Beverly Park, Mira Road East, we offer a picturesque escape for your special occasions. With a capacity ranging from intimate gatherings of 300 to grand events of up to 2000 people, Seven Seasons Lawn caters to weddings, sangeet ceremonies, religious functions, and more. Our sprawling lawn provides ample space for your guests to mingle, dance, and create cherished memories. Equipped with air-conditioned rooms and modern washroom facilities, we prioritize the comfort and convenience of our guests. Additionally, our expansive car parking area ensures hassle-free arrival and departure for all attendees. At Seven Seasons Lawn, we understand the importance of every detail in making your event unforgettable. From the moment you step onto our grounds, our dedicated team is committed to providing exceptional service and assistance, ensuring that your vision is brought to life seamlessly. Located at Kanakia Rd, opposite Santhome Public School, near Seven Eleven Club, our venue is easily accessible from various parts of Mira Bhayandar and beyond. Whether you're planning an intimate affair or a lavish celebration, Seven Seasons Lawn is the perfect destination for your next event. Come and experience the magic of Seven Seasons Lawn, where every season is filled with joy, love, and unforgettable moments.
